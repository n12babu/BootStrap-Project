About CandyLand

What if an eccentric chocolatier and a daredevil pop star had a whirlwind romance, got married while skydiving, and had a glamorous, glittering love child who grew up to rule a small nation? Welcome to CandyLand, where colossal candyfloss constructions meld with a tantalizing taffy twistedness!
